Explorable Breakdown

The Visual Explorable

A large, high-contrast donut chart that lets you tap categories to drill down. Focuses on visual hierarchy and instant understanding of proportions.

  • Instantly see big vs small expenses
  • Interactive - tap to see what's inside a category
  • Very clean and modern aesthetic
📊
Comparative Narrative

The Spending Story

Uses horizontal bars and comparison cards to show how this month compares to your average. Focuses on "Am I spending more than usual on this?"

  • Easier to read exact values than circles
  • Provides context (vs average, vs last month)
  • Identifies trends automatically
🎯
Budget Tracking

The Goal-Oriented Hub

Sets spending limits for categories and tracks progress. Focuses on control and staying within limits.

  • Directly actionable: "I have 500k left for Food"
  • Visual progress bars (Green/Yellow/Red)
  • Proactive alerts for overspending

My Recommendation

I recommend The Visual Explorable combined with Category Drill-down. It fixes your readability issues by moving labels to a clean legend and provides deep insight by letting you see the specific transactions that caused a category to be high.
